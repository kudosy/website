What Kids Actually Learn

Five money behaviors.
One childhood.

Instead of teaching your kids about good financial habits, help them live it.

💪
EARN

Kids learn that value comes from effort and contribution. Every Kudo is a lesson: I did something worthwhile and was recognized for it. The foundation of a work ethic.

🎯
SAVE

The goal system teaches delayed gratification — one of the most powerful predictors of adult success. Set a target. Watch progress. Feel the satisfaction of earning something real.

🛒
SPEND

Using kudos helps your kids experience real purchasing decisions with limited resources. Fictional currency, but real world application. Spend now on something small, or save for what you really want? That's economics at age 7.

🤝
GIVE

Kids can give Kudos to siblings — teaching generosity alongside earning. When a child gives from what they've earned, something beautiful happens.

📈
INVEST

The magic of a fictional currency is that kids can lock Kudos for a set period and get them back with interest. You set the duration and the interest rate, they learn a life-long lesson about making money work for them.

Goal Progress System

FAQ

Everything you're wondering

What is Kudosy and how does it work?
+
Kudosy is a family reward app that uses a fictional currency called Kudos to gamify good behavior and teach children financial literacy. Parents award Kudos to children for any behavior they want to recognize — chores, kindness, talents, service. Children save Kudos toward goals in the Treasure Chest reward store. No bank account or real money is involved at any point.
Does Kudosy require a bank account or real money?
+
No. Kudosy uses a completely fictional currency called Kudos — no bank account, no debit card, no real money. This is one of Kudosy's biggest advantages over apps like Greenlight and BusyKid. Your children get all the financial education and none of the financial risk. When your child is ready to redeem their Kudos for a reward, you approve it in the app with a tap — and the Kudo cost is automatically deducted from their balance. From there, it's up to you: pick up the item at the store, order it online, or hand it over from a stash you've already prepared. Kudos are a family currency, not a payment system. You stay in complete control of what gets purchased, when, and how much it costs.
What ages does Kudosy work for?
+
Kudosy works for any age, starting from 3 years old. Each child gets their own card, goal, balance, and character. You award Kudos differently to a 5-year-old than a 13-year-old — different behaviors, different amounts, different rewards. The same app works for your whole family simultaneously.
Can I reward my kids for things other than chores?
+
Absolutely — this is one of Kudosy's most important features. You can award Kudos for anything you value: character, kindness, service, practicing a talent, helping a sibling, doing well in school, or showing courage. Kudosy can be used to reward anything.
How is Kudosy different from Greenlight or BusyKid?
+
Greenlight and BusyKid require real bank accounts and debit cards — which creates fraud risk, frozen card situations, billing complaints, and regulatory complexity. Kudosy uses fictional currency, so all of those problems are simply impossible. Kudosy also rewards any behavior (not just chores), works from age 3, and costs one flat price for your whole family.
How much does Kudosy cost?
+
Kudosy is $5.99/month or $49.99/year for your entire family — unlimited children. There is a free 7-day trial. You can cancel anytime from your iPhone Settings. No credit card is required for your children to participate.
How does the investment feature work?
+
You set the investment terms per child — a lock period (e.g., 14 days) and a return rate (e.g., 20%). When your child invests Kudos, their balance decreases by the invested amount. On the payout date, their Kudos are returned with interest automatically. This teaches money growth through direct experience, with no real money at risk.
